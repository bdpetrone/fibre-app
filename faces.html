FACES OF FIBRE

Brianne Petrone

Meet Brianne, the first in our community of creators and personalities behind Fibre. After joining the team in February 2020, she’s produced video content for Fibre’s social channels, helped with brand identity/strategy, created marketing teasers and promos. Passionate about visual storytelling, fashion, and creating impactful content, Fibre has been a perfect outlet.

Ben Chipman

Meet Ben, our wantonly optimistic Face of Fibre. Passionate about people and connections, Ben brings a unique perspective on all things lifestyle and fashion. Ben's goal with his content is to build connections, inspire intimacy, and create community (through oversharing on the internet). Doing it all with personality and style, you don't want to miss out on more from Ben.

Erin Fehr

Meet Erin. Your favorite slow fashion icon & nyc fashion girl. A midwesterner turned F.I.T. student, Erin has found her niche in the world of ethical fashion, and what it truly means to embody that. With a huge passion for sustainability in different areas of life, Erin lives and breathes the fashion industry. Stay tuned for more city, lifestyle, and fashion content from Erin, all through the lens of sustainability.
